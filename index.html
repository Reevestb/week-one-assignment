Why you should get a cat??

A cat has the ability to both calm your nervous system and provide an immediate outlet for fun and play.
Although cats are independent animals who like to scavenge and explore on their own terms, they are also
very affectionate with their owners and people they trust.

Why should you get a dog??

Dogs provide us with a sense of emotional wellbeing thanks to the unconditional love they give
which is why they're also great company and comfort for people who live alone.
A man's best friend as they say!

Are exotic pets for you??

Sometimes known as “exotic pets”, non-traditional companion animals (NTCAs) can be very challenging
to keep as pets due to their complex welfare needs. These needs include: a suitable environment and diet;
being able to display their natural behaviours; and being housed with or without other animals as needed.
